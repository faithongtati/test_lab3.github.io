Home > Traditions > Folk dance

Whether you’re wearing a dirndl or lederhosen, a smart suit or
casual attire, your clothes are irrelevant. Bavarian dancing is first and
foremost about having fun. This is why many dances are simple and
easy to learn, even for beginners. Sometimes it’s enough just to watch
for a while to pick up the basic steps. The Bavarian dance culture
invites everyone to get involved and become one with the sound of the music.
