The primary school is the first and common school. It comprises
years 1 to 4 and has the objective to support all students in their
personal development. At the centre is the objective to facilitate the
acquisition of knowledge, initiate understanding, develop interests,
promote social modes of behaviour and to support musical and
practical skills, and structure value systems.
